Storm Response

Storm Damage Restoration

Central Florida averages a significant hurricane or tropical storm impact every 2–3 years. When it hits, the contractor you call first determines how well your insurance claim goes.

What We Do

From First Call to Final Restoration

Storm damage creates urgency — and urgency is exactly when most homeowners make decisions they regret. Don't sign anything with your insurance company before having a qualified roofing contractor assess the full scope of damage. The first adjuster estimate is rarely the full picture.

We respond promptly, protect your property from further damage with emergency tarping where needed, document everything thoroughly with photos and written reports, and then walk through the inspection with your insurance adjuster — making sure nothing is missed.

Our team understands how insurance claims work, what documentation adjusters need, and when a supplemental claim is warranted. We've helped Central Florida homeowners recover significantly more than their initial settlement offers.

Emergency tarping to prevent further interior damage
Full photo and written damage documentation
Insurance claim filing assistance
On-site adjuster meeting and advocacy
Supplemental claim support when initial offers are low
Complete restoration under one contract
We work with all major Florida carriers

Warranty Coverage

Storm restoration projects carry our 5-year workmanship warranty on all new installation work. Material warranties are provided by the manufacturer of the replacement system installed.
