FAQ

Common questions from Florida homeowners.

Are you licensed and insured in Florida?

Yes. We hold an active Florida roofing contractor license and general liability insurance. Engineering credentials are also maintained in-house as a licensed engineering firm.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes. We provide written damage documentation, work with insurance adjusters, and help coordinate the claim through approval and construction. We do not act as a public adjuster, but we support the process thoroughly.

How quickly can you respond to storm damage?

We prioritize urgent storm-related calls and work to get on-site within one business day for emergency inspections when conditions allow.

Do you offer financing options?

We understand that major roofing work is often unexpected. Ask us about financing options when you request your consultation and we will walk you through what is available.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Central Florida and primarily serve Orange, Osceola, Seminole, Lake, and Polk counties. Contact us for service availability in surrounding areas.

What makes you different from other roofers?

The combination of roofing contractor license and engineering capability in one firm means structural issues found during roofing jobs are resolved by our team rather than handed off to a third party or left for the homeowner to figure out.
