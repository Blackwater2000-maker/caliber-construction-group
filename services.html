What we do

Roofing systems and specialty construction services for premium Florida clients.

Every service we offer is backed by our Florida roofing license, premium materials, and an expert team, and a boutique project management model built for discerning property owners.

Residential roofing

Premium systems for high-value homes across Central Florida.

Tile roofing

Concrete and clay tile installation, repair, and full replacement. Ideal for Central Florida estates and Mediterranean-style architecture. Engineering review included for load compliance.

Standing seam metal

Premium standing seam metal systems for modern, contemporary, and high-wind-rated applications. Exceptional longevity with minimal maintenance requirements.

Designer shingles

Architectural and impact-rated shingle systems from leading manufacturers. Strong insurance premium reductions and Florida product approval compliance.

Flat and low-slope

TPO, modified bitumen, and built-up systems for flat roofs on homes, additions, and commercial properties. Proper drainage design and warranty documentation.

Repair and leak response

Emergency and scheduled repair services for all roof types. Detailed diagnostics, photo documentation, and written scope before any work begins.

Storm restoration

Full insurance claim support including inspection, wind damage documentation, adjuster coordination, and rebuild to pre-storm or improved condition.

Commercial and specialty

Selective commercial roofing for boutique clients.

HOA and multi-family

Coordinated replacement programs, reserve study support, and consistent quality standards for homeowner associations and multi-family communities.

Hospitality and commercial

Boutique hotels, restaurants, churches, and professional office buildings. Minimal disruption scheduling and premium material options.

Maintenance programs

Annual inspection and maintenance memberships to extend roof life, catch problems early, and maintain warranty compliance on premium roofing systems.
