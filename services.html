Stone-Coated Metal Tile

The most advanced roofing system for Florida homeowners who want the look of clay tile without the weight, brittleness, and maintenance. Stone-coated metal tile — from brands like Boral Steel, DECRA, and Gerard — uses a structural steel core coated in natural stone granules to replicate the profile of Spanish barrel tile, Mediterranean flat tile, or wood shake.

75% lighter than concrete tile, hurricane-tested to Florida Product Approval standards, and backed by 50-year warranties. Qualifies for wind mitigation insurance discounts.
