Flexible Financing Options

A new roof is rarely a planned expense. We offer financing options that let you protect your home now without waiting for budget timing to align.

Payment Options

Roofing That Fits Your Budget

We've partnered with leading lenders to make quality roofing accessible to every homeowner — regardless of when the expense hits. Most applicants receive a decision the same day.

0% Interest Promotional Period

Qualifying homeowners can take advantage of a 0% interest promotional period — allowing you to pay for your roof over time without additional interest cost if paid in full before the promotional period ends.

Low Monthly Payment Plans

Spread the cost of your roof replacement into manageable monthly payments. Terms vary based on credit profile and project size — we'll walk you through the options at your free inspection.

Same-Day Decisions

We know urgency matters — especially after storm damage. Most financing applications receive a decision the same day so you can move forward quickly when your home needs it.

Insurance Claim + Financing

When your insurance settlement covers part of the cost and you need to finance the remainder, we coordinate both — making the transition from claim approval to completed roof seamless.

State Program

My Safe Florida Home Program

The My Safe Florida Home Program, administered by the Florida Department of Financial Services, provides qualifying homeowners with a free wind inspection and matching grants of up to $10,000 for hurricane-hardening improvements — including qualifying roof upgrades.

For every dollar you invest in eligible improvements, the state provides matching grant funding. Caliber Construction Group is a registered participating contractor. Contact us to check your eligibility — the program has specific income and property requirements.
