Our Story

A Different Kind of Roofing Company

Caliber Construction Group was built around a simple idea: invest in highly skilled crews, use the best materials, and deliver a result that speaks for itself.

Who We Are

Built on the Belief That Quality Demands Attention

Caliber Construction Group is a Central Florida-based boutique roofing contractor — meaning every client gets the focused attention and quality of a small, dedicated team without the limited capacity that usually comes with it. Our well-trained crews handle the full range of residential and commercial roofing work, and our dedicated job-site managers oversee every project from start to finish.

Our operating model is lean by design. Less overhead means more of your investment goes toward what actually matters: skilled crews, premium materials, and a dedicated manager on every job site making sure the work is done right.

We serve homeowners and commercial property owners throughout Orange, Seminole, Osceola, Lake, Polk, and surrounding counties in Central Florida.

Our Standard

What We Stand For

Dedicated On-Site Oversight

Every project has a dedicated manager physically present on your job site — overseeing the crew, inspecting material installation, and ensuring every detail meets our standard.

Premium Materials Only

We don't offer a "budget line." Every system we install comes from leading manufacturers with proven Florida performance records and strong warranty programs.

Boutique Client Experience

You'll always know who is working on your roof, what they're doing, and why. No call centers. No runaround. Direct access to the people responsible for your project.

Honest Assessments

If your roof can be repaired, we'll tell you. We don't pitch unnecessary replacements. Our business is built on referrals — and referrals come from trust.

Permit-Proper Installation

Every project is permitted, inspected, and documented. You receive full permit records and warranty documentation at project completion — protecting your investment and your property record.

Licensed & Fully Insured

Florida-licensed roofing contractor with full general liability and workers' compensation coverage. Our permit holder also holds a Florida civil engineering license.
