Licensing and credentials

What our dual-license model means for your property.

Florida Roofing License

Covers all residential and commercial roofing scopes including new installation, replacement, repair, and storm restoration across the state of Florida.

Engineering License

Provides structural analysis capability, proper load calculations, wind uplift documentation, and engineering-stamped plans when required by permit offices or insurance adjusters.

Construction Group License

Allows us to take on broader exterior construction scopes beyond roofing, including structural repairs, decking, fascia, soffits, and envelope improvements.
