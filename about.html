Our story

A construction company with boutique roofing at the center of the brand.

Caliber was built to serve Central Florida homeowners and property professionals who want premium quality, honest communication, and the premium confidence that comes from having both a roofing license and an Florida roofing license in the same company.

Who we are

Craftsmanship, precision, and a selective client model.

We take on fewer projects so we can serve each one better. Every roof we install or repair is reviewed against craftsmanship standards, not just roofing code minimums. That means better detailing, better documentation, and a finished product that performs longer in Florida's extreme weather environment.

Our construction license lets us address premium issues discovered during roofing scopes, which most roofing-only contractors have to subcontract out or leave unaddressed.
