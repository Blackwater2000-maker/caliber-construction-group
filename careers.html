Field Specialist

Central Florida Field / On-Site Mon – Fri + select weekends Competitive pay
Apply now Response within 2 business days

What you’ll do

  • Execute roof installations, repairs, and storm restoration work on residential and selective commercial properties across Central Florida.
  • Perform pre-installation inspections and document existing conditions with photos and written notes.
  • Install shingle, tile, metal, and flat roofing systems per manufacturer specs and Florida building code.
  • Coordinate on-site with the project lead, engineering team, and occasionally insurance adjusters during storm restoration projects.
  • Maintain a clean, organized, and safe job site at all times — Flagstone Heritage projects are held to a higher standard of site professionalism.
  • Complete post-installation walkthroughs and assist with final documentation before client sign-off.

What we’re looking for

  • Minimum 2 years of hands-on roofing field experience in Florida or comparable climate conditions.
  • Demonstrated experience with at least two of the following: architectural shingles, concrete or clay tile, standing seam metal, TPO or modified bitumen flat roofing.
  • OSHA 10 certification required; OSHA 30 preferred. Safety harness and fall protection compliance is non-negotiable.
  • Valid Florida driver’s license and reliable transportation to job sites in the Central Florida area.
  • Ability to work outdoors in Florida weather conditions, including heat, humidity, and post-storm environments.
  • Strong communication and reliability. We run a boutique operation — if you’re committed, we’re committed to you.
